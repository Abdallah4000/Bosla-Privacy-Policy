Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 26, 2026

We built Bosla to help you organize your day and stay on top of your prayers. Your privacy matters to us — here's a plain-language summary of how the app works.

We don't collect your data

Bosla works entirely on your device. There are no user accounts, no sign-in, and no personal data sent to us during normal use.

  • No accounts or login required
  • No ads
  • No tracking or analytics

Everything stays on your device

Your tasks, schedules, reminders, and settings are saved locally on your phone. Nothing is uploaded or synced anywhere.

Location — for prayer times only

Bosla asks for your location to calculate accurate prayer times for where you are. That's it.

  • Your location is never sent to us or any server
  • It's used only to calculate prayer times on your device
  • You can skip this and set your location manually

Sending feedback

If you choose to send us feedback, the message you write (and any optional photo or email you attach) will be sent to us so we can respond and improve the app.

This is completely optional and only happens when you tap "Send".

This is the only moment any information leaves your device.

Permissions we ask for

Here's why we need each permission:

  • Location — to calculate prayer times
  • Notifications — to remind you of tasks and prayers
  • Alarms — so prayer and task alarms ring at the exact right time
  • Run in background — so alarms still work when the app isn't open
  • Internet — only used if you send feedback

Changes to this policy

If we ever add features that change how your data is handled, we'll update this page before those features launch.

Questions?
