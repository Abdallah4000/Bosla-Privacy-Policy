Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-03-26

Bosla is a Time Boxing / task management app that helps you plan your day, manage recurring tasks, and shows prayer times and alarms. This policy explains what data the app accesses, what leaves your device, and why.

Data We Collect

Bosla does not maintain user accounts and does not collect personal data as part of normal app usage.

  • No user accounts or login
  • No advertisements
  • No third-party analytics or tracking SDKs
  • No background data sync to any server

Data Stored on Your Device

All information you enter — tasks, schedules, reminders, prayer preferences, and app settings — is stored locally on your device using Android's standard storage. This data never leaves your device during regular use.

  • Tasks and timeline schedules
  • Reminder and alarm configurations
  • Prayer calculation settings (method, madhab)
  • App appearance and notification preferences

Location (for Prayer Times)

Bosla may request access to your device's precise location to automatically calculate accurate prayer times based on your coordinates.

  • Location is used only for this on-device calculation via the Adhan library.
  • Your coordinates are not sent to any server.
  • Location data is not stored beyond the current session.
  • You can deny this permission and enter your location manually instead.

Feedback Submissions

Bosla includes an optional Send Feedback feature. If you choose to submit feedback, the following data is sent to our server:

  • Feedback type (bug report, feature request, etc.)
  • Subject and message text you write
  • Star rating (if provided)
  • Contact email address (optional — only if you choose to include it)
  • Any images you voluntarily attach to the report
This is the only time any data leaves your device. Feedback submission is entirely optional and user-initiated. No data is sent automatically.

Submitted feedback is used solely to improve the app and is not shared with third parties.

Permissions Requested

Bosla may request the following device permissions:

  • Location (ACCESS_FINE_LOCATION / ACCESS_COARSE_LOCATION) — to calculate prayer times automatically.
  • Notifications (POST_NOTIFICATIONS — Android 13+) — to display task reminders and prayer alerts.
  • Exact Alarm (SCHEDULE_EXACT_ALARM — Android 12+) — to deliver alarms precisely at the scheduled prayer and task times.
  • Full-Screen Intent (USE_FULL_SCREEN_INTENT — Android 14+) — to show alarm screens on the lock screen when a prayer or task alarm fires.
  • Foreground Service (FOREGROUND_SERVICE) — to keep alarm delivery reliable while the app is in the background.
  • Internet (INTERNET) — used only when you submit feedback. Not used during normal app operation.
All permissions are used exclusively for the features described above and nothing else.

Network Access

Bosla checks network connectivity status to determine whether a feedback submission can be sent. No app data is transmitted over the network unless you actively use the Send Feedback screen.

Background Processing & Alarms

To ensure prayer alarms and task reminders fire reliably — even when the app is not in the foreground — Bosla may run an Android Foreground Service and schedule system alarms using Android's AlarmManager API.

  • This processing happens entirely on your device.
  • No personal data is accessed or transmitted during background processing.
  • The foreground service shows a persistent notification while active, as required by Android.

Children's Privacy

Bosla does not knowingly collect any personal data from children under 13. Since the app collects no personal data under normal use, it is safe for all ages.

Changes to This Policy

If Bosla adds new features in the future — such as cloud sync, user accounts, or third-party integrations — this privacy policy will be updated before those features ship. The "Last updated" date at the top will always reflect the most recent revision.

We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
